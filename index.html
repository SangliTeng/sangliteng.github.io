Sangli Teng

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Berkeley (Berkeley AI Research), advised by Prof. Koushil Sreenath. I received my Ph.D. in Robotics from the University of Michigan in 2025, advised by Profs. Maani Ghaffari and Ram Vasudevan. I received my B.Eng. in Mechatronics from Chongqing University in 2020.

Email  /  Scholar  /  X (Twitter)  /  GitHub

Research

I'm interested in motion planning, optimization, geometric control, dynamics, and their intersection with machine learning and differential geometry.
